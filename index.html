No Child Left Behind (NCLB) was one of the most impactful educational polices ever passed. It had the posibility of being THE policy that determining what the legacy of the the Bush administration was going to be. I myself am a product of the NCLB policy and wanted to explore why it didn't achieve what it was made for. Why the strategies that it used didn't tacked and fix the issues that we still see in out public education system. And when taking a look at those reasons and strategies how it took affected, and in some cases took away, childs Human Right to Education.
